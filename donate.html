Donate To The Arizona Pirate Party

You can help us ensure and protect the rights and liberties we stand for by making a simple donation. Even just a single dollar can help us in a great way.

Since we are a political organization, we will be transparent that we are subject to rules put fourth by the Federal Elections Commission (FEC), meaning that we must:

  • Collect your name and address for bookkeeping purpose.
  • If you contribute more than $200 in a calendar year, then the FEC requires us to list you as a donor in our campaign finance reports. In addition to reporting your name and address, we also have to report your employer and occupation.

It's extra bookkeeping, yes, but we believe it is a good measure of transparency that we commit to that other areas of politics lack.

Warning: We do not accept donations from business entities or political action committees. They will be refunded.
